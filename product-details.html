CrunchCoCo - Product Details

What is CrunchCoCo?

CrunchCoCo is more than just a breakfast snack – it's a modern solution for healthy, fast, and eco-friendly eating. Developed by NETLE Enterprise, CrunchCoCo is designed for people who are constantly on the move but still prioritize their health and environmental impact.

Each CrunchCoCo bar contains a handcrafted blend of grains, nuts, and dried fruits, carefully selected to offer both nutritional value and rich flavor. The ingredients are bound together with natural honey and coconut oil, ensuring a wholesome experience with every bite. Wrapped in edible glutinous rice paper, CrunchCoCo is not only delicious but also sustainable — there's no packaging waste, making it ideal for eco-conscious consumers.

The snack is highly versatile: you can eat it dry as a crunchy bar, or soak it in warm milk like a traditional cereal. It’s perfect for students, busy professionals, travelers, and even kids. With CrunchCoCo, healthy eating becomes easy, enjoyable, and responsible.

Key Ingredients

  • Rolled oats and whole grain cereals
  • Dried fruits such as cranberries, bananas, and raisins
  • Almonds, sunflower seeds, and chia
  • Glutinous rice paper (edible wrap)
  • Natural honey and coconut oil

Features and Benefits

  • Zero-waste packaging: Everything is edible, even the wrap!
  • Nutritious and energizing: Packed with fiber, healthy fats, and protein
  • Convenient: No need for utensils or bowls
  • Multi-style consumption: Eat dry for crunch or soak in milk for a cereal-style treat
  • Perfect for all ages: Students, professionals, or kids

Our Mission

We believe that a good meal shouldn't harm the planet. CrunchCoCo is our answer to sustainable snacking — it’s not just food, it’s a lifestyle. We are committed to environmental responsibility and health-conscious innovation.
